About Joy

Joy Love is a Mexico City based artist. She was raised in Mexico City, surrounded by landscapes and nature, which heavily inspired her style. Her main subjects are florals, foliage, space, lettering, earth, symbolism, portraits, and a few spiritual pieces here and there. The artist enjoys working with watercolor, ink and acrylics on paper. You can find Joy in her studio, painting, managing her online sales and teaching workshops.
